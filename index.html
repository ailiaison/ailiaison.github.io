CargoX eBOL / eBL as the document backbone

The platform uses CargoX-style secure document envelopes as the single source of truth for bills of lading, letters of credit, insurance and compliance certificates. AI Liaison Officer builds deals from logistics, risk and finance parameters and then automatically composes and sends standard envelopes via API.

  • Standard envelopes with structured metadata and attached eBOL / trade docs
  • Immutable document hashes mirrored into Web3 TradeAgreement contracts
  • Document lifecycle events streamed into the orchestration layer

CBDC rails: e-Cedi × Digital Ruble corridor

Inspired by EMTECH’s modern central banking infrastructure and Ghana’s e-Cedi pilots, AI Liaison Officer treats CBDC as programmable settlement rails for ECOWAS ↔ Russia trade.

e-Cedi rail (Ghana / ECOWAS)

Institutional wallets and CBDC movements exposed via EMTECH-style APIs. The backend orchestrator can allocate, hold and release e-Cedi balances per shipment and per route.

  • Wallets for ECOWAS energy & commodity exporters
  • Programmable holds linked to CargoX envelopes
  • Events mirrored into smart contracts

Digital Ruble rail (Russia)

On the Russian side the system integrates with bank / central bank APIs for Digital Ruble, synchronizing CBDC settlements with logistic and legal events.

  • Settlement for Russian importers & refiners
  • Alignment with national CBDC regulations
  • Bridging ECOWAS and Russian banking corridors

Hybrid & FX layer

Where CBDC is not yet available, the orchestrator falls back to classic trade-finance, tokenized claims and FX rails, keeping the same unified interface.

  • Letters of credit, guarantees, forfaiting
  • Tokenized receivables on Web3 networks
  • Risk-weighted routing of payment flows

Web3 mesh: Trade Agreements on programmable networks

Inspired by libp2p’s modular networking stack, the corridor treats TradeAgreement contracts as a mesh of programmable commitments referencing CargoX documents and CBDC payments.

  • On-chain TradeAgreement with eBOL hash, parties and milestones
  • Tokenized payment claims and risk tranches for institutional investors
  • Oracle-driven updates from CargoX, e-Cedi and Digital Ruble rails
